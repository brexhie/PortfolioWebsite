So, who am I?

My name is Brett Ryan. I'm a software developer from Uitenhage, Eastern Cape who loves to design and build web based solutions.

I'm studying a 3 year Software Development course at CTU Training Solutions in Port Elizabeth. My studies conclude at the end of October 2025 after the submission of my final Work Integrated Learning project with an outcome of a National Certificate in Information Technology: Database Development, NQF 6, SAQA ID 71850. I'm planning on studying towards a Bachelor of Science in Computing at UNISA, starting 2026.

Although I have studied a variety of technologies in college, my core skills include C#, ASP.NET Core, Java, and SQL Server.
