So, who am I?

My name is Brett Ryan. I'm a software developer from Kariega, Eastern Cape who loves to design and build web based solutions.

I'm studying a 3 year Software Development course at CTU Training Solutions in Gqeberha. When I'm not coding, I love playing my favourite video game Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege, reading, training MMA with my buddies, and going to the gym.

My passion for tech started in my first class of Computer Applications and Technology (CAT) in grade 10 of high school. In grade 12, I wrote my first HTML web page. This was the moment I knew I wanted to become a programmer.
